Biography

Feroz Ahmed graduated from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology (KUET), Bangladesh in 2007 with his Bachelor in Electrical and Electronic Engineering before pursuing his official Master program in photonics at Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC), Barcelona, Spain in 2012. He received his official Master degree in Photonics, offered by Institute of Photonic Sciences (ICFO) , Barcelona, Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), Universitat de Barcelona (UB) and Universitat de Autonoma (UAB), Barcelona, Spain. Currently he is doing his PhD in medical bioengineering dept. of Okayama University, Japan at Terahertz image sensing laboratory. His research work in the Master program was on optical bio-sensing at visible light range in the field of biophotonics to develop applications in Biomedical Engineering. His current research interests are microfluidic chip fabrication, fluid dynamics in microfluidics and Terahertz signal and image processing.
